CONTACT US

Welcome to Clarity Counts.

We equip and empower you to act wisely and freely on change that you desire or that is forced upon you, through our mentoring, coaching, facilitation, and mediation practice for individuals and teams.

If you...

  • Need an independent sounding board for ideas
  • Want more clarity on what you want to do or what you are good at
  • Need change in your project, job, venture, relationship or life
  • Need guidance on how to respond to what has happened to you
  • Are in a conflict and want to prevent further escalation
  • Want to dissolve incongruency between what you want and should do
  • Want to take action now, but its too vaugue to know how to start

Then contact us to discover how we can help you find answers

At Clarity Counts we believe that

  • You get best results and enjoy life most when you are yourself
  • You and the people around you are interdependent
  • You are choosing what you spend your time, energy, and money on
  • You are the solution to problems and issues that cross your path
  • Our professional techniques, tools, and processes are means helping you to take action towards your goals
  • Your head, heart, and hands have a role in your choices, decisions and actions
  • Asking for help is a sign of strength

At Clarity Counts we specialise in empowering you (individual or team) to

  • Be yourself at all times, and enjoy the benefits of doing that
  • Appreciate the 'as-is' state as the perfect base to move from towards the 'to-be' state
  • Drive results you want, leveraging people and experiences that cross your path

We do that by

  • Equipping you to get to know yourself better, and to clarify your interests and goals
  • Grounding your goals, so you truly believe they can happen
  • Clarifying with you how you can best move forward towards satisfying fulfilment
  • Enabling you to overcome fear, shame, or guilt that may be distracting you
  • Connecting you to others if you want to enrich or accelerate your journey
  • Clarifying with you the expectations you have of others around you

Whether you are at the start of something new, in a void, in the midst of turmoil, at cross roads, or at what seems the end of the road, we’d love to help you elicit yoursustainable truth for your next step, so you can move forward with confidence.

We identify with you what’s alive in you. We work with you through defined andproven processes to get results. We work as a coach, mentor, facilitator, investigator, mediator, or conciliator - the role we take when we work with you is determined by where you are in your journey and your needs.
